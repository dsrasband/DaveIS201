Faith

This is What I Believe

I am a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, and as such firmly believe that Jesus Christ is my personal Savior and Redeemer. I believe that God is my Heavenly Father, that He loves me, and that He sent His Son Jesus Christ here to Earth to atone for my sins and make it possible for me to return to live with Him and and my family forever. Following Jesus has completely changed me, and I believe that by building my life upon the principles of His gospel I can find lasting happiness in this life and the next. I believe that this gospel was taught by the Savior while He lived upon the Earth, and that the Lord restored that same gospel to the earth in the early 1800's through a modern prophet, Joseph Smith, and we can learn about this gospel through inspired scripture, such as the Bible and the Book of Mormon. I'm so grateful for the knowledge and beliefs I have, they have brought me so much peace and comfort in my life. I'm happy to talk with anyone about my beliefs, and if you have any questions, don't hesitate to reach out or click the links below.
